About

I am a masters student at IIT Hyderabad. Prior to this I worked as a network infrastructure engineer at Akamai Technologies. I completed my undergraduation from B.V.B college of engineering and technology, Hubli. I also had a short stint as project assistant at IIT Mandi, where I worked on predicting network failure from log files using machine learning algorithms. I am interested in pursuing research in the area of computer vision and natural language processing. I grew up in Hubli, which is the second largest city in Karnataka. During my free time, you can find me curating new recipes or experimenting with my culinary skills.
